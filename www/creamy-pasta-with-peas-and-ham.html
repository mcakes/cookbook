Creamy Pasta with Peas and Ham

creamy pasta
Serves: 2 Time: N/A

Ingredients

  • 2.0slicebacon
  • 1.0mediumonion (fine dice)
  • 200.0gramspasta
  • 0.75cuppeas
  • 1.0cupheavy cream
  • 1.0tsplemon zest
  • 0.25cupchicken stock
  • 0.25cupparmesan

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until not quite al dente (time to complete with sauce). Reserve 1 cup pasta water. 2. Cool bacon over medium. Remove with slotted spoon (leaving fat).
  2. Add onion, stirring until soft.
  3. Deglaze with chicken stock.
  4. Add peas.
  5. Add cream. Bring to boil.
  6. Add pasta and pasta water. Maintain rapid simmer until sauce is thickened and pasta is al dente
  7. Add bacon and lemon zest.
